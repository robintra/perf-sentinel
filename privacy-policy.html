Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy is taken seriously. This page describes, in plain terms, the (few) data this site processes, in line with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

No data collection

This site is purely informational. It has no form, no user account, and no newsletter signup. No personal data is collected directly by the publisher.

Cookies and trackers

This site sets no cookie and uses no tracker or audience-measurement tool (no Google Analytics, Matomo, Plausible, etc.). No consent is therefore required and no cookie banner is needed.

Local storage (preferences)

Your display preferences (light/dark theme and language) are stored in your browser's local storage. This information stays on your device, is never sent to a server, and you can clear it at any time by clearing the site data in your browser.

Fonts

Fonts are hosted directly on the site. No request is sent to a third-party service (no Google Fonts), so no data is transmitted as a result.

Hosting and logs

Like any web host, GitHub Pages may record technical connection data (IP address, browser type) for operational and security purposes. This processing is GitHub's; see GitHub's privacy statement.

External links

The site links to third-party services (GitHub, crates.io, docs.rs, LinkedIn) which have their own privacy policies. The publisher is not responsible for their practices.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify and erase your data. As the site holds no personal data about you, such requests mainly concern the host. For any question: robin.trassard@gmail.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the CNIL, the French data-protection authority (cnil.fr).
