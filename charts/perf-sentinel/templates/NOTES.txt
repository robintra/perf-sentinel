Heads up: workload.replicas is {{ .Values.workload.replicas }}. perf-sentinel keeps
per-trace and correlation state in memory, per pod, with no shared state. A plain
round-robin Service splits one trace's spans across pods and silently degrades N+1
and correlation detection. Either run replicas=1, or route trace-aware: consistent
hashing by trace_id via the OTel Collector loadbalancingexporter. See
docs/HELM-DEPLOYMENT.md ("Scaling and state").
